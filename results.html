Performance Visualization

Key Findings

  • Transfer Learning: YAMNet embeddings achieve 83.4% test accuracy vs. 16-47% for traditional features, representing a 35+ percentage point improvement
  • Feature Preprocessing: Normalization improves average accuracy from 28% to 31% for traditional classifiers
  • Best Model: YAMNet+RF (SSL, TH=0.90) achieves 83.8% test accuracy, the highest overall
  • Traditional ML: Random Forest (46.8%) and XGBoost (44.5%) perform best among traditional methods
  • Self-Supervised Learning: Pseudo-labeling provides 0.3-0.4% improvements over baseline YAMNet+RF
